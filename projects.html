    Hearth

    AI-powered weekly meal planner

    Hearth turns a few questions into a week of dinners and the grocery run to match. A guided questionnaire captures allergies, diet, tastes, effort and budget, then Claude generates a structured meal plan — using forced tool-use for clean JSON and prompt caching to keep it fast and cheap. From there it builds an AI recipe library and an auto-generated grocery list priced against your chosen store (e.g. King Soopers), budget-aware and kept in sync: change a preference and Hearth flags the now-stale plan or list and offers to regenerate. Accounts and saved plans sit behind NextAuth login.
